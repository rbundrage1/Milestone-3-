Rules & Scoring

Downs & Distance

  • You get 4 downs to gain 10 yards.
  • If you gain 10 or more you get a new first down.
  • On 4th down you usually punt or try a field goal.

Clock (very short)

  • 4 quarters, timeouts, and the clock stops on some plays.
  • 3 timeouts per team each half.
